About Flexion U

Flexion U (Flexion Universe) is the structural research environment built on the Flexion Framework. It formalizes how systems arise, evolve, generate time, preserve viability and terminate through collapse. Every Flexion discipline — from the Flexion Immune Model and NGT Protocol to Flexion Motion Lab — uses the same structural core: X = (Δ, Φ, M, κ).

Flexion U is not a metaphor or a brand only. It is a complete structural language for designing systems that can remain alive — biological, economic, algorithmic, cognitive, or social.
