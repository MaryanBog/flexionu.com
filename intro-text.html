Flexion Universe

Flexion Universe is a unified structural framework for describing how systems remain viable, lose reversibility, and collapse.

Instead of modeling behavior or outcomes, Flexion models the structural state that determines whether recovery is still possible.

Any system — biological, economic, technical, or informational — can be described through a single living state vector:

X = (Δ, Φ, M, κ)
Δ — deviation · Φ — structural energy · M — memory · κ — viability

This gives one language for stability, drift, irreversibility, and collapse boundaries — independent of domain.
